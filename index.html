  • Helpr

    This is a group project that I worked on, called "Helpr". The idea behind this app is to create a place where users can go to ask for help, or volunteer their time. For example, you may have an elderly neighbor who needs help with yard work. You can go on Helpr and post a new project. Then other users can sign up to volunteer for the project. I worked on all pieces of this group project. I created the majority of the design and front-end. I wrote RESTful routes for our database. I helped another student in my group with our Google Maps API.

  • Technologies Used

    HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, Express, Node, MySql
